1.1.0:
  Changed namespace to sfmidi.
  Changed naming conventions of functions to camelCase.

  Dropped SFML 1.6 support.

  Minor bugfix for compiling with SFML 1.6 (in Music.cpp).

1.0.1:
  Added support to preload midi files.
